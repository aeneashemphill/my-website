About Me


My journey into tech began with a career in the performing arts. I'm a playwright, screenwriter, actor, musician, and so much more.

But, as many know, this is a tough way to pay the bills. With my industry decimated by COVID-19, I needed a new path, one that would let me use my passion, creativity, and finely-honed collaborative skills. Tech turned out to be the perfect fit.

I dove into codeacademy and Skillcrush courses and used my quarantine to learn valuable skills. Now, I develop online platforms that suit your needs and truly represent you.
